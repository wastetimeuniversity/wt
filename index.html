💎 Enhancements
Randomised boost multipliers applied to each in-game currency - one of the most impactful progression systems in Waste Time.
✦ Enhancement System in Action
Last Updated: 18 May 2026, 5:30 PM (UTC)
Enhancements are a randomised multiplier system in Waste Time that applies a permanent boost to the gain rate of most in-game currencies. Once unlocked at 1 Sx Time in Chapter 1, most currencies you own receives its own enhancement slot. You spend a Reroll to randomly assign a new tier to a slot, and a Lock to protect a slot you're happy with, from future rerolls. Tiers range from the common F (1×) all the way up to the best P+ (49.99×) - a multiplier difference that can compress days of grinding into hours. The two highest tiers, P and P+, are gated behind the Chapter 2 Upgrade Tree, making that upgrade a significant progression milestone. Managing your Rerolls and Locks strategically - prioritising high-tier rolls on your most active currencies and using Locks to protect them - is one of the highest-leverage habits in the game.
✅ Unlocked at 1 Sx Time - Chapter 1 🔒 P & P+ require CH2 Upgrade Tree 🪙 Applies to all in-game currencies 📊 10 tiers total
💎 What Are Enhancements?
🎲
Randomised Boosts
Each currency gets a randomly assigned enhancement tier when you Reroll. The tier determines the multiplier applied to that currency's gain rate.
🔒
Locking Good Rolls
Found a great tier? Spend a Lock to preserve it. Locked slots are untouched by future rerolls, letting you protect high-value enhancements.
🔑
Unlock Condition
The Enhancement system unlocks in Chapter 1 once you reach 1 Sx Time. Every currency you subsequently unlock will receive an enhancement slot.

⚠️ High Impact System
Enhancement multipliers are extremely impactful - the gap between F-tier and P+-tier on a primary currency can save hours or even days of grinding. Prioritise locking high-tier rolls on your most-used currencies first.
💡 Pre-CH2 Milestone
Without the Chapter 2 Upgrade Tree, the highest tier you can roll is S++ (14.99×). Getting S++ across all active currencies before CH2 is a strong progression target, if you have enough Locks and Rerolls.

🎲 Getting Rerolls & Locks
Multiple sources exist for both Rerolls and Locks. Listed below from most to least effective for consistent free gain.
🎟️
Redeem Codes  BEST FREE SOURCE
Active codes frequently bundle large numbers of Locks and Rerolls. Check the Codes tab on this site and redeem every code the moment it drops - this is consistently the best free source of both resources.
🎪
Event Drops  HIGH YIELD
Participating in the ongoing in-game event regularly drops significant quantities of Locks and Rerolls. Staying active during events is one of the fastest ways to stockpile both.
📋
Daily Quests  HIGH YIELD
Daily quests reward a substantial amount of Locks and Rerolls on completion. Completing your quests every day accumulates into a sizeable stockpile over time.
📺
Watch Ad - Free Rewards
Available directly inside the Enhance menu. Watching an ad rewards +6 Rerolls and +3 Locks for free. Availability varies by region and time.
⏱️
Passive Base Rate [ONLY IN CHAPTER 1]  SLOWEST
Rerolls and Locks accrue passively around the clock, but the base rate is slow. Don't rely on this as your primary method - treat it as a bonus during long idle sessions.
🎲 1 Reroll every 5 min 🔒 1 Lock every 20 min
🛒 Shop & Game Passes
Rerolls and Locks can be purchased with Robux (⬡) in the Enhance shop (Can be opened from the Enhance menu). The Best Deal tiers offer the highest value per Robux spent.
📺

Watch an ad from the Enhance menu to earn free resources. No purchase required.
