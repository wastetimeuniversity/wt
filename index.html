💎 Enhancements
Randomised boost multipliers applied to each in-game currency - one of the most impactful progression systems in Waste Time.
Last Updated: 28 May 2026, 5:30 PM (UTC)
✦ Enhancement Area
Enhancements are a randomised multiplier system in Waste Time that applies a permanent boost to the gain rate of most in-game currencies. Once unlocked at 1 Sx Time in Chapter 1, most currencies you own receives its own enhancement slot. You spend a Reroll to randomly assign a new tier to a slot, and a Lock to protect a slot you're happy with, from future rerolls. Tiers range from the common F (1×) all the way up to the best P+ (49.99×) - a multiplier difference that can compress days of grinding into hours. The two highest tiers, P and P+, are gated behind the Chapter 2 Upgrade Tree, making that upgrade a significant progression milestone. Managing your Rerolls and Locks strategically - prioritising high-tier rolls on your most active currencies and using Locks to protect them - is one of the highest-leverage habits in the game.
✅ Unlocked at 1 Sx Time - Chapter 1 🔒 P & P+ require CH2 Upgrade Tree 🪙 Applies to most in-game currencies 📊 10 tiers total
💎 What Are Enhancements?
🎲
Randomised Boosts
Each currency gets a randomly assigned enhancement tier when you Reroll. The tier determines the multiplier applied to that currency's gain rate.
🔒
Locking Good Rolls
Found a great tier? Spend a Lock to preserve it. Locked slots are untouched by future rerolls, letting you protect high-value enhancements.
🔑
Unlock Condition
The Enhancement system unlocks in Chapter 1 once you reach 1 Sx Time. Every currency you subsequently unlock will receive an enhancement slot.

⚠️ High Impact System
Enhancement multipliers are extremely impactful - the gap between F-tier and P+-tier on a primary currency can save hours or even days of grinding. Prioritise locking high-tier rolls on your most-used currencies first.
💡 Pre-CH2 Milestone
Without the Chapter 2 Upgrade Tree, the highest tier you can roll is S++ (14.99×). Getting S++ across all active currencies before CH2 is a strong progression target, if you have enough Locks and Rerolls.

🎲 Getting Rerolls & Locks
Multiple sources exist for both Rerolls and Locks. Listed below from most to least effective for consistent free gain.
🎪
Event Drops  HIGHEST YIELD
Participating in the ongoing in-game event regularly drops significant quantities of Locks and Rerolls. Staying active during events is one of the fastest ways to stockpile both.
🎁
Daily Rewards  CONSISTENT
Logging in each day and collecting your Daily Reward grants Locks and Rerolls among its rotating drops. Streak bonuses on later days (such as Day 7) increase yields significantly.
⏱️
Playtime Rewards  HIGHLY REWARDING
The Playtime Rewards system grants bundles of Locks, Rerolls, and other items as you accumulate time in-game. Check it regularly to claim milestones as they unlock.
📺
Watch Ad  FREE REWARDS
Available directly inside the Enhance menu. Watching an ad rewards +6 Rerolls and +3 Locks for free. Availability varies by region and time.
🎟️
Redeem Codes  EXTRA SOURCE
Active codes frequently bundle large numbers of Locks and Rerolls. Check the Codes tab on this site and redeem every code the moment it drops - this is consistently the best free source of both resources.
⏱️
Passive Base Rate [ONLY IN CHAPTER 1]  SLOWEST
Rerolls and Locks accrue passively around the clock, but the base rate is slow. Don't rely on this as your primary method - treat it as a bonus during long idle sessions.
🎲 1 Reroll every 5 min (+2 every 5 min with 2x Rerolls gamepass) 🔒 1 Lock every 20 min

🏆 Special Ranks
Rare cosmetic titles earned through limited-time events - each granting always-on passive boosts to your core currencies.

✦ Special Ranks System
Special Ranks are rare cosmetic titles earned through limited-time events and event crates in Waste Time. Unlike regular ranks, each Special Rank carries permanent passive percentage boosts to Time, GrandTime, and Fragments - bonuses that are always active, even when the rank isn't equipped as your displayed title. Crucially, boosts from all ranks you own stack additively with each other, making every new rank a meaningful upgrade regardless of which one you have equipped. Older ranks obtained during past events are marked as Legacy and can no longer be obtained through normal means.
🏆 Earned via Limited Events ⚡ Always-On Passive Boosts ✦ All Owned Ranks Stack 🎟️ Obtained from Event Crates
✦ What Are Special Ranks?
🎟️
Earned Through Events
Special Ranks drop from Event Crates during limited-time events. Crates come in Common, Rare, Special, and Event tiers - each with a different pool of possible ranks.
⚡
Always-Active Boosts
Every rank you own applies its passive % boosts to Time, GrandTime, and Fragments at all times - no equipping required. Equipping only changes your visible title.
✦
Stacking Bonuses
Boosts from all owned ranks stack additively. Owning multiple ranks compounds your passive income - every rank you earn is a permanent upgrade.
⚡ Why Special Ranks Matter
Boost currencies
Time, GrandTime, Fragments
Boost type

⚠️ Time-Limited System
Special Ranks are only available during active events. Legacy ranks from past events cannot be re-obtained - if you missed them, they're gone. Participating in every new event is strongly recommended.
💡 Stacking Strategy
Since all rank boosts stack additively, collecting every rank you can - even low-rarity ones - adds up meaningfully over time. Prioritise higher-rarity ranks for bigger individual gains, but never skip a free rank.
🎟️ Current Event - Crates

The current event features a Crates system where you open crates of different rarities to win Special Ranks and other rewards. Crates come in Common, Rare, Special, and Event tiers - each with a different pool of possible ranks.

📖 How to Obtain & Equip Special Ranks
1
Open Crates
Earn crates through the active event or from the Playtime Rewards section. Tap the More button in-game, then open the Storage icon and open your crates. You may receive a Special Rank as a reward.
2
Equip the Rank
After receiving a Special Rank, return to Storage, locate the rank, and tap it. Select Equip to wear it as your displayed title.
3
Boosts Are Always Active
You do not need to equip a rank for its boosts to apply. All passive boosts are active at all times, regardless of which rank (if any) is currently displayed.
⚡ Equipping a rank only changes your visible title - it does not affect your passive boosts in any way.

🎮 Gamepasses
Permanent perks, quality-of-life upgrades, and chapter-specific multipliers - all purchased once with Robux.
Last Updated: 28 May 2026, 5:30 PM (UTC)
Gamepasses are permanent one-time purchases made with Robux (⬡) that provide lasting benefits throughout your playthrough. Unlike most other progression upgrades, gamepass effects persist across resets and chapters - once bought, the perk is yours forever. Chapter-specific passes only apply to their relevant chapter, while miscellaneous passes benefit you across the entire game.
⬡ Purchased with Robux ♾️ Permanent one-time purchase 📖 Chapter & global passes available 🎮 Applies across resets
⚠️
Regional Pricing Notice
▾
Gamepass prices shown use ⬡ Robux values as listed in-game. Actual costs may be higher or lower for some of the gamepasses depending on your region and platform due to regional pricing being enabled. We have added a warning icon to indicate when this is the case.
🎲
Exclusion of Locks and Rerolls
▾
Gamepasses related to 🔒 Locks and 🎲 Rerolls have been excluded from this section due to comparative irrelevance in the scope of the larger in-game mechanics. Refer to the "Shop and Game Passes" section of the Enhancement tab for information regarding these.
